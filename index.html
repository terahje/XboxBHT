Game Reviews

Remembering myself as a kid when the first Xbox came out, I grew up an avid sports gamer as they were the most justifiable purchases in a time less fortunate than now. While Madden, NBA Live, NBA 2k, College Football, and FIFA keep me busy, I’ve always loved a single-player story. I remember playing the first Fable and falling in love with the decision-making aspect of the game, that choices could either make you good or evil. I remember getting lost for hours in San Andreas, although with cheat codes, the first GTA I finished. With these great memories comes the recollection of dreed after finishing a game and realizing that sixty dollars are gone, sometimes never to be played again. That’s where Microsoft and Xbox seriously raised the bar with the addition of Game Pass Ultimate, improving an already solid Xbox Live experience. Through Game Pass, I’ve had access to numerous games I would never have purchased. A majority of those games I ended up loving, so much so that I decided to build this page. I will only highlight games I’ve played and enjoyed my time with the game. Unique curation of games and a perspective I feel is missing amongst the reviews I frequently watch. If you like any of the games previously mentioned, you’re in the right place.

Hardware Reviews

As a lover of gadgets, what a time to be alive! Being one of the skilled lucky ones, I was able to get my hands on the Series X from day one and have enjoyed the new generation for almost a year now. I felt an honest hardware review section of the items I use would help cut through the noise that can cloud these decisions to invest in your overall experience. I feel obligated to share my experience, as when I first got the Series X, my tv was from 2011. To take full advantage of this box, I’ve scoured all of YouTube, countless trips to Best Buy to ensure what I got was quality. I always tell people I like to buy things once, meaning I don’t want to buy a cheaper item that will break two or three times and end up costing the same amount as the more expensive, better-built option. That also doesn’t mean to get the most expensive item, just the best quality whenever possible. While I have not tried every headset or Tv, I feel enough research was done on each item to confidently endorse my choices. If you have questions on controllers, headsets, Tv’s, or sound systems, I got you!
